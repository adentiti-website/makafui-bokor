A Vision for a Safer Future

"Bridge" explores the dynamic interplay between advanced technologies such as AI, quantum computing, and IoT, and the vulnerabilities they introduce. Through real-world stories, expert insights, and actionable guidance, the book equips readers to navigate the challenges of the modern cybersecurity landscape.

Key Themes Explored

  • Emerging Threats: A deep dive into the latest trends, from AI-driven cyberattacks to the vulnerabilities of IoT devices and quantum computing's dual-edged potential.

  • Industry-Specific Strategies: Tailored insights for sectors like healthcare, finance, retail, and education, highlighting unique risks and solutions.
  • Everyday Cybersecurity: Practical steps for SMBs and individuals to protect their systems, data, and livelihoods.
